Culture of Oman

Muscat

The capital city of Oman, Muscat offers stunning beaches, historic forts, and lively markets. Perfect for culture lovers and first-time visitors.

Nizwa

Nizwa is known for its ancient fort, bustling souq, and rich Omani heritage. Dive into traditional crafts, spices, and local cuisine.

Wahiba Sands

Immerse yourself in the desert life of Wahiba Sands. Enjoy camel rides, sandboarding, and traditional Bedouin hospitality.
